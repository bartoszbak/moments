## What Moments stores

Moments is designed so most of your content stays on your device. This may include:

- moment titles
- optional descriptions
- dates
- manifestation settings
- saved AI-generated reflection or manifestation text
- app preferences such as colors, icons, widget settings, and reminder settings
- photo backgrounds you choose

The app also stores limited widget data locally in the shared app group container so the widget can display your selected moments.

## Permissions

Moments may request access to:

- Photos, if you choose a photo background
- Calendar, if you enable calendar sync
- Notifications, if you enable manifestation reminders

These permissions are optional. If you do not grant them, the related features will not work.

## AI features

If you use AI reflections or manifestations, the text needed to generate that response is sent to third-party AI providers through OpenRouter. This may include your moment title, optional description, date context, and whether the moment is past, upcoming, or a manifestation.

The generated response may be saved locally on your device as part of the moment.

## Subscriptions

If you purchase or restore Moments Plus, subscription and purchase-related information is processed by Apple and RevenueCat. I do not receive your full payment card details.

## Calendar sync

If you enable calendar sync, Moments can read your available writable calendars, let you choose one, and create, update, or delete calendar events for eligible moments.

## Notifications

If you enable manifestation reminders, Moments can schedule recurring local notifications on your device. Notification content may include your moment title and saved guidance text.

## Sharing

I do not sell your personal information. I do not use Moments for cross-context behavioral advertising.

I may share information only with service providers needed to run app features, including:

- OpenRouter and downstream AI model providers
- RevenueCat
- Apple services used for payments, calendar, notifications, widgets, and photo selection

## Retention

Most app content remains on your device until you edit it, delete it, clear app data, or remove the app. Third-party providers may retain data they process according to their own terms and policies.

## Your choices

You can:

- delete moments in the app
- remove photo backgrounds
- disable calendar sync
- disable reminders
- decline permissions in iOS
- manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple
- remove the app from your device

## Children's privacy

Moments is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law to provide valid consent on their own.
